~Quick Highlight

History

Nepal has a glorious history shaped by kings and dynasties. Its monuments and traditions still reflect the past today.

Festival

Dashain and Tihar are the biggest festivals in Nepal. They bring happiness, family bonding, and colorful celebrations.

Food

Nepali food like dal bhat and momo is widely loved. It shows the culture, taste, and warmth of the people.

Music

Nepali music is rich in folk traditions and melodies. It connects generations and expresses the spirit of Nepal.

~Quick Facts Related Culture of Nepal

Religious Harmony

Nepal is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and home to both Hindu and Buddhist traditions that coexist peacefully.

Diverse Ethnic Group

Over 120 ethnic groups live in Nepal, each with their own festivals, music, and traditions.

Folk Music & Dance

Traditional instruments like the madal, sarangi, and bansuri are central to Nepali folk culture.

Festivals All Year

Nepal celebrates hundreds of festivals annually, with Dashain and Tihar being the biggest nationwide.
